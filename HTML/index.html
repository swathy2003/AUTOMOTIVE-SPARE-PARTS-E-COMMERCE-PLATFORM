About Us

New Tech Auto components Pvt ltd Was started in the year 2002 as a Partnership firm and moved into become a private limited company in 2011 onwards. We manufacture wide range of Auto Parts / Components to OEMs for more than 18 years, operating in Chennai, India (hub for automobile & auto parts industry in international trade) By combining state-of-the-art facility and technology with a highly skilled and motivated work force, NEWTECH endeavors to supply automobile products in highest quality, at competitive price. Apart from producing Auto Parts/Components of consistent quality and upholding satisfaction of all, NEWTECH considers it as a duty to be socially responsible organization and ensure that all parts are environmentally friendly. NEWTECH capabilities span wide range of products in automotive sector and the company stands as a perfect amalgam of people, technology and resources reinforced with strategic top management with thorough knowledge of nuances of automotive industry
